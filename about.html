    • About Us

    We are a dedicated group of students from the Michigan State University AI Club and Wayne State Cyber Defense Club who are trying to solve real world problems by combining the power of Artificial Intelligence and Cyber Security into projects like Mackenzie. We made this project to help people who are having technical issues in categories such as Hardware, Software, Network, Security, etc. We designed this chatbot to be able to give users the correct answers to common I.T. questions. Our answers are from freely-available reputable online sources, and we always share the source with each response.


    Our Goals

    We understand that technical issues can be very annoying and disrupt productivity so our goal with Mackenzie is to provide instant and accurate answers to user questions and lessen the amount of time spent browsing the web for answers or waiting in a helproom. We also know that comprehending technical information can be very difficult so we made sure our solutions were designed in step-like format and straight to the point.
